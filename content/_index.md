# About the launcher.

HyPrism is a free and open source third party launcher for Hytale, built and maintained by the community.
It exists to give players control, transparency, and a clean experience without hidden restrictions.

The project focuses on user freedom, openness, and long term maintainability.
There is no vendor lock in, no forced telemetry, and no artificial limitations imposed on users.

HyPrism aims to stay simple where possible, powerful where necessary, and approachable for contributors.
Everything is developed in the open, with community discussion and review guiding decisions.

The goal is simple.

Make launching and managing Hytale easy.
Make the launcher hackable and extensible.
Make sure users are respected.

## What HyPrism offers?

- Fully open source launcher developed in the open.
- Cross platform support for Linux, Windows, and macOS.
- Community maintained releases and updates.
- Clean and minimal interface without unnecessary bloat.
- Privacy first design with no forced analytics.
- Designed to be moddable, forkable, and extensible.
- Modern interface with light, dark, and system themes.
